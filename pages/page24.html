Like a ship following the light from a lighthouse, you know that your lady will always be your guiding star. You can always depend on her to guide you through difficult times. One day, you receive a message from your lady. She has gone on a journey to a far away land, and she has invited you to come with her. She tells you that you will have to brave treacherous beasts and dangerous paths in order to find her, but she promises that you will be rewarded greatly when you find her.

Her offer is tempting, but it does sound pretty risky. What if you don't make it back?
