Strangely enough, your fellow knights aren't too eager for you to publicly read off a list of the Queen's conquests, perhaps because half of the knights are on the list. No one accepts your request to bear testimony of the Queen's misdeeds.

Undeterred, you begin compiling a list of every rumor and secret liaison you can think of. You're sure that the King will have to pardon you in face of all of this evidence.

Unfortunately for you, you never make it to the trial. One of the accused knights took some initiative and poisoned your food to make sure you couldn't spill any secrets.

Snitches get arsenic.
