You know that the only person who could have convinced the King that you were a traitor is your very own lady. Your heart burns with anger as you think about her betrayal. Thankfully, when you are taken before the court, you are able to convince the King of your own loyalty and of the falseness of the rumor he heard.

You are acquitted and allowed to return home to your estates.

Hurt and saddened by the betrayal of the women you loved, you

Plot your revenge: your lady will learn that she cannot treat you this way

Compose mournful poetry about the loss of your greatest love.
