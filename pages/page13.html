You love your lady, but she's not your highest priority. You have a kingdom to serve! Promising him a large island, you send your most loyal vassal, Sancho Panza, after your love with every confidence that she will be returned safely. Several weeks pass and you have been busy with important activities such as riding around the countryside, reading love poetry, and sighing with longing as you think about your love. One morning, as you are napping under the shade of an apple tree, you hear a rider approach. It is the loyal Sancho Panza with your lady, freshly rescued in tow. Your lady, eager to be reunited, leaps off the back of the horse and starts to run towards you.

Overjoyed at the sight of your lady, you

Play a love song on your set of pipes, then recite an epic poem you composed in her honor.

Glare at her coldly and accuse her of infidelity.
