You run her through with your sword and black blood gurgles out of her body. Her body seems to melt away. You cheer in victory. You turn to release the knight, but he's already disappeared. So have all the webs. And the cave. Where the spider-woman once stood, stands your lady! It seems that you broke the enchantment that imprisoned her in this terrible form!

You embrace her, feeling a little guilty that you had just ran her through with a sword, but she doesn't seem to be bothered. She says that she missed you and that she is so glad you finally came to rescue her.

The two of you gallop home on your noble steed and are married on the morrow.

And even if you're sometimes weirded out by your lady's new fascination with venomous spiders, you never mention anything about it.

Some things are best not to know.

THE END
