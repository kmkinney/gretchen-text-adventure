Your lady impatiently acquiesces, but only on the condition that you recite quickly. You agree, and even offer to cut out the 31st canto to save time. You wake up in a pasture having no memory of the past few weeks. You do notice a set of reed pipes around your neck, as well as a large flock of sheep grazing peacefully.

For some reason, you feel as if a weight has been lifted off your shoulders, and that you can finally relax.

You and your sheep life happily ever after!
