Simple words cannot begin to describe the beauty and elegance of your love. As noble as an angel, as dazzling as a fairy, as sweet-tempered as a shepherdess, your love is the personification of generosity, gentleness, and benevolence. You would fight a hundred battles, defeat a thousand foes, and--most importantly --compose a million verses in honor of your love. You would sooner stare death in the face than say a single word to deface her good name.

The first time you spoke to your love

You promised each other your undying devotion (Lanval)

She guided you through a difficult time in your life (Dante)

You have never actually spoken to your love directly--she is far too pure for that (DQ)
