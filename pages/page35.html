You quickly and quietly leave the cave, trying to forget everything that happened. You return straight back to your home village and sell all of your armor to buy a large pasture with several sheep to graze in. The salesman threw in a set of reed pipes for your trouble.

Sometimes, when the days are long and the sheep are peacefully grazing, you think about your lady. You never did find her, but you don't exactly feel bad about it. Adventuring isn't all it's cracked up to be, and your lady probably wasn't really as beautiful as you thought she was.

Besides, every time you picture her face, all you can envision are two glittering mandibles.

THE END
